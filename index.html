About
77 @ Anjarle

Anjarle is one of the finest places in konkan having all basic facilities. Just 18 km from Dapoli, Anjarle has a clean, unspoiled beach with white sand & is surrounded by coconut trees.

77 @ Anjarle is not just a 9 acre cluster of fully developed N.A. plots but is also blessed with scenic beauty. It's a soothing getaway where you can rejuvenate and enjoy with your family & friends. An investment that will never let you down.
